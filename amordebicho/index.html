Amor de Bicho

This project is a digital product that works as a hub for animal adoption NGOs, enabling listings, veterinary follow-up, and a data dashboard for NGOs. It was developed as a study project through a design challenge page focused on UX and UI.

Goal 1: Propose a digital product that increases NGOs' adoption rates

Goal 2: Make the adoption process faster

Goal 3: Create an up-to-date report on the rescued animal’s health

Goal 4: Give NGOs more visibility

1. Understand

Using a CSD Matrix, I was able to map how much I knew about the topic, my view on adoption, and how NGO adoption processes work.

The questions below guided the research (which was conducted only as desk research due to the short development timeframe of the challenge).

- What are the biggest needs of NGOs focused on animal rescue?
- What is done when an NGO has no visibility?
- What digital channels currently exist for adoptions by NGOs or veterinary clinics?
- Which social groups adopt animals most often?
- Which diseases most affect rescued animals?
- How are treatments performed for rescued animals?
To answer the questions above, desk research was used in order to meet the challenge deadline.

Mind Map technique, used to visually organize the information considered in this context.

How is treatment carried out for rescued animals?

Even if the animal is dirty, ideally it should first be taken for a medical evaluation. The veterinarian can determine whether the animal is fit for bathing and grooming and assess its health condition.

Artigo Petz, 2019

General Summary

Insights

It is important to inform potential adopters about diseases that street animals can transmit to humans, to raise awareness about preventive care and hygiene.

The adoption process usually involves documentation and an adoption fee, which can be a barrier for some people. This process needs to be simplified to make adoption easier.

Financial resources, veterinary support, shelters, food, volunteers, outreach, and medicine are the main resources NGOs need to rescue and care for animals.

The internet is one of the most effective ways for NGOs to gain visibility and engagement. It is important to have a marketing strategy and knowledge of social media boosting tools.

Young women with completed elementary education and no fixed income are the group that most supports adoption and opposes buying pets.

It is important to guide adopters about the health condition of rescued animals and the need for a medical evaluation before bathing and grooming.

Information Taxonomy

Marketing and visibility strategy

3. Create

By aligning the research results with the goals defined at the beginning of the project, it was possible to create requirements that serve as a basis to evaluate the generated ideas and how each one meets those requirements. Below are some ideas generated through brainstorming.

Chosen idea details

It will be an online platform service for NGOs to list their animals, where users can choose from an animal database.

There will be two types of users who access the platform: the NGO and the adopter. It must cover NGO registration, animal listings, and the adopter stage during adoption.

The tool will list the resources used to support adoption and evidence that proves the actions took place.

Service Blueprint

Its objective is to understand and visualize the entire user experience regarding a specific product or service. Because it can be used by teams, it also aligns everyone involved, making it possible to identify problems or opportunities in the project.

Visual Design

Painel Semântico

Based on the concepts explored in the mind map technique, a semantic panel was created for the interface to guide how visual elements and colors should behave in the project. The concept keywords are: love, lightness, and comfort.

Pink tones will be used as the main colors, reinforcing the concepts of love and comfort. Lightness in the interface makes navigation more fluid, as it allows action elements to guide user decisions, while white and gray support this navigation. Visual elements and components should also have rounded edges, reducing the “formal” look of the interface and supporting interaction with the content inside these components.

5. Validate

Resultados

I had the opportunity to run the research in person with one participant. By observing them, it was possible to notice key points.
